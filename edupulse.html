EduPulse: Navigating Student Pathways – Dropout Data Decoded.


"EduPulse: Navigating Student Pathways – Dropout Data Decoded" is a comprehensive project aimed at illuminating the complex dynamics of student dropout rates within the education system. The title "EduPulse" signifies a rhythmic and active engagement with educational insights, echoing a deep commitment to understanding the student experience.




ANALYSIS OF DROPOUT

Analyzing student dropout involves a comprehensive examination of various factors that contribute to students leaving an educational program before completion. This analysis helps identify patterns, reasons, and potential solutions to reduce dropout rates and improve educational outcomes..



Data Collection



Gather data on student enrollments, demographics, academic performance, attendance, and reasons for leaving from educational institutions, government databases, surveys, or research studies.

Analyze dropout rates based on various demographics such as age, gender, ethnicity, socio-economic status, and geographic location to identify disparities.



Investigate the relationship between academic performance, grades, standardized test scores, and dropout rates to determine the impact of academic struggles.

Attendance and Engagement Analysis



Analyze attendance records and student engagement levels to understand the correlation between attendance patterns and dropout . Organize focus group discussions involving stakeholders to delve deeper into specific aspects related to student dropout, gaining a qualitative understanding of the issue.
